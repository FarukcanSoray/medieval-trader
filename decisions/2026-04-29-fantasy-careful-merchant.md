# Player fantasy is the careful merchant

## Decision
The player's fantasy is the careful merchant — a calculating accountant who feels smart when a route pays off. Mastery is in reading the system (prices, distances, risks) and committing to the route the numbers support, not luck or reflexes.

## Reasoning
This frames all future design decisions about information transparency, feedback quality, and the shape of the decision space. It rules out hidden information, real-time pressure, and combat-as-skill.

## Alternatives considered
The Director offered other fantasies (risk-taking adventurer, route-puzzle solver). The user selected careful-merchant explicitly.

## Confidence
High. Explicit choice; foundational to the pillars and scope.

## Source
Director intake, 2026-04-29; user selected option (a) in response to the player-fantasy question.
