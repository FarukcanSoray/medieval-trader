# One of each system, no variants

## Decision
The scope holds to one map generator, one price model, one event system, one save slot, one death screen. No multiple biomes, trader archetypes, or post-death meta-layers within the project.

## Reasoning
Director's third tension resolution. The brief acknowledges ambitious scope for a first AI-developed solo project and commits to "one of each" as the finish line, with polish and tuning the last step — not multiplication of systems. The Scope Critic later reinforced this: integration tax compounds with each additional system variant.

## Alternatives considered
Multiple competing systems (multiple price models, regional event systems, biome-flavoured map generators). Implicitly rejected by the scope frame and by the integration-tax concern.

## Confidence
High. Explicit commitment in the brief; reinforced by the Critic's review.
