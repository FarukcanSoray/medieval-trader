# Death rare and earned, not roguelite cadence

## Decision
Death is rare and earned. A competent player's first death lands hours in, usually from compounded bad decisions or an ignored risk — not from a single unavoidable roll. Old age is a real ending. The game rejects roguelite reset-spam cadence.

## Reasoning
Director resolved the tension between "careful merchant fantasy" and "permadeath" by saying mastery is in-world to this trader on this map, not meta across saves. Death is the punctuation that gives the run weight; if it comes too often, mastery becomes generational speed-running rather than careful accounting.

## Alternatives considered
Roguelite cadence (frequent runs, fast resets) was the path not taken. The Director flagged this tension explicitly during intake and resolved it.

## Confidence
High. Explicit tension resolution with clear reasoning; sits in the brief.
