# Slice-4 = encounters slice (chosen from three remaining candidates)

## Decision
Slice-4 is the encounters slice. Bandit-only scope; weather/spoilage/tolls deferred to slice-5+.

## Reasoning
Slice-3 close-out playtest signal was "not fun yet." Three slice candidates remained on the board (encounters / goods catalogue expansion / aging). Encounters won because: (a) "not fun" mapped to "no stakes" — encounters add risk to known routes, directly serving Pillar 2 (travel always costs something the player feels); (b) slice-1 §10's "wait until end-to-end loop exists" precondition is now satisfied (slice-3 closed); (c) the carryover from [[2026-04-29-slice-zero-encounters]] is fully consumable.

## Alternatives considered
- **Goods catalogue expansion** (2 → 6-12 goods) — stronger attack on decision sparseness, but cheaper-feeling than risk introduction.
- **Aging / second death cause** — activates lifespan tuning knob; serves Pillar 3 but doesn't address "not fun yet" directly.

## Confidence
High. Explicit user ratification ("encounters slice is good") on Director's recommendation.

Director intake at start of slice-4 session.
