# Slice-3 = pricing slice (chosen from four candidates)

## Decision
Slice-3 is the pricing slice. It carries the open free-lunch owe-note from slice-2.5, directly serves Pillar 1 (every trade decision is a math problem the player can win), and unblocks tuning of `TRAVEL_COST_PER_DISTANCE`.

## Reasoning
Director was prompted with four candidate scopes (pricing / encounters / goods catalogue / aging) and chose pricing because the carryover from [[2026-05-02-slice-2-5-free-lunch-deferred-to-pricing-slice]] explicitly named it. The owe-note is scoped, ready to close, and the choice directly advances the kernel's load-bearing tension.

## Alternatives considered
- Encounters slice — deferred subsystem, no open owe-note
- Goods catalogue expansion — content + UI scaling, smallest scope
- Aging / second death cause — would activate the lifespan tuning knob

## Confidence
High. Explicit user ratification ("indeed, pricing slice it shall be") on Director's recommendation.

Director intake at start of session.
